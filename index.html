Visualization colors demo

First row shows colors, while the second shows the error from the expected luma. The columns are: traditional biconic HSL, spherical HSL, luma-corrected, and spherical luma-corrected.

The luma-corrected colors are simple to compute and the result is fairly smooth even when the colors are saturated. They are simple enough that the approach should not be novel, but I have not found a reference yet.
